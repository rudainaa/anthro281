About Us

A 23 person class at Haverford College

This website was created in response to a lack of centralized information online surrounding the Gwich’in people and the fight against oil drilling projects. We are a group of learning students, not experts, putting back into the public a compilation of loosely annotated resources that we’ve found. We approach this project with a sense of humility around our own sense of positionality and acknowledge that we do not have all the answers. Our goal is to make the information that exists online more accessible to anyone and everyone who seeks it.
